## Concept 1: "The $1,000 Journey" (RECOMMENDED)

### Hook (0-3s)
- Black screen. A single glowing **$1,000** animates in from particles
- Text slams in: **"What if $1,000..."**
- Quick camera shake / glitch effect

### Problem (3-10s)
- The $1,000 gets **locked inside a cage/vault** (representing overcollateralization)
- Text: **"In DeFi, you lock $1,000..."**
- The vault shrinks to show only **$700** accessible
- Text: **"...just to use $700"**
- Red tint, frustrating UI elements flying around (scattered tabs, multiple wallets)

### Transformation (10-20s)
- Violet energy pulse **shatters the vault**
- Vanna logo reveal with gradient glow
- The $1,000 **multiplies** — numbers cascade up: $2,000... $5,000... $10,000
- Animated leverage slider (like the website) slides from 1x to 10x
- Text: **"Vanna gives you 10x"**

### Product Showcase (20-40s)
- Website screenshots animate in with parallax depth:
  1. **Deposit UI** slides in → ETH selected, $1,000 entered
  2. **Capital Multiplier** → slider animates to 10x, number counts up to $10,000
  3. **Strategy Builder** → blocks snap together (Spot Buy + Perp Short)
  4. **Portfolio Dashboard** → numbers animate, health factor bar fills green
- Each screenshot has a subtle 3D tilt/perspective with glassmorphism reflection

### Social Proof (40-48s)
- Protocol logos orbit in a circle (Hyperliquid, Derive, Uniswap, Pendle, Morpho...)
- Text: **"13 Protocols. One Account."**
- Backer logos fade in row by row (Pivot Ventures, Draper University, Stellar Foundation, Gitcoin)

### CTA (48-55s)
- Gradient text: **"The Future of DeFi is Composable"**
- Vanna logo + tagline: **"Leverage Anything & Anywhere"**
- Buttons animate: **"Launch App"** | **"Join Waitlist"**
- Particle burst outro

## Visual Effects & Techniques (For All Concepts)

### Typography Animations
- **Slam/Impact**: Text scales from 200% to 100% with overshoot spring
- **Typewriter**: Characters appear one by one with cursor blink
- **Glitch**: Text briefly scrambles then resolves (great for hooks)
- **Gradient Reveal**: Text fills with brand gradient left-to-right
- **Word-by-word**: Each word fades in sequentially with slight upward motion

### Transitions Between Scenes
- **Wipe with gradient**: Violet-to-red gradient sweeps across
- **Particle dissolve**: Scene breaks into particles, reforms as next scene
- **Zoom through**: Camera zooms into a UI element that becomes the next scene
- **Morphing**: One shape smoothly transforms into the next
- **Glitch cut**: Quick static/glitch frame between scenes

### Screenshot Showcase Techniques
- **3D Perspective Tilt**: Screenshots float with subtle 3D rotation (CSS perspective)
- **Parallax Layers**: UI elements at different Z-depths move at different speeds
- **Highlight Pulse**: Key numbers/features get a glowing pulse effect
- **Cursor Animation**: Animated cursor clicks through the UI to show interaction
- **Zoom Focus**: Camera slowly zooms into key parts of each screenshot

### Color & Mood
- **Background**: Deep black (#111111) with subtle noise texture
- **Accent Glows**: Violet/Red/Electric Blue ambient lights
- **Particle System**: Floating dots in brand colors for atmosphere
- **Gradient Overlays**: Subtle gradient mist across scenes
- **Light Leaks**: Violet/red light leaks at transitions

### Sound Design Notes (for later)
- Bass-heavy intro hit
- Whoosh sounds on transitions
- Subtle electronic ambient pad
- Click/snap sounds on UI interactions
- Ascending tone during "10x" multiplication
- Cinematic impact sound on logo reveal

## Recommended Approach

### Primary Choice: **Concept 1 ("The $1,000 Journey")**

**Why?**
1. **Relatable hook** — everyone understands $1,000
2. **Clear problem→solution narrative** — DeFi locks capital vs Vanna multiplies it
3. **Product showcase** — directly uses website screenshots (already available)
4. **Numbers-driven** — $1K→$10K is the most powerful visual Vanna has
5. **Easiest to execute in Remotion** — mostly 2D animations + screenshot reveals

### Hybrid Option: Combine Best Elements
- **Hook** from Concept 4 (10x explosion — maximum hook energy)
- **Problem/Solution** from Concept 1 ($1,000 journey — clear narrative)
- **Product Showcase** from Concept 1 (screenshot parallax — shows real product)
- **Social Proof** from Concept 4 (fast ticker — efficient use of time)
- **Outro** from Concept 6 (constellation compression — memorable brand moment)
